Executive Founder
Brief biography

Plas-tech's co-founders first met at the Mombasa Plastics Prize hackathon, where our paths aligned with a common goal: finding innovative solutions to tackle the pressing plastic waste issue in Mombasa. Through engaging capacity-building masterclasses on ideation, entrepreneurship, and prototyping, we realized our shared passion for sustainability and the environment. Together, we formed a dynamic team, combining our expertise in environmental engineering, sustainable practices, and business development. As a cohesive unit, we co-founded Plas-tech with the mission to convert plastic waste into clean cooking gas, aiming to make a positive impact on the world around us. This journey began at the hackathon, and now, as co-founders of Plas-tech, we are dedicated to driving sustainable change, inspiring a greener future, and contributing to a cleaner planet.
