Our projects


At Plas-tech, we are passionate about driving innovative solutions to tackle the pressing global issue of plastic waste. Through our dedicated efforts and cutting-edge technology, we have initiated impactful projects that demonstrate our commitment to a sustainable and cleaner future.

Plastic Waste Conversion Technology


Our flagship project focuses on developing state-of-the-art technology to convert plastic waste into clean cooking gas. By harnessing the power of advanced processes, we are transforming plastic waste into a valuable resource while significantly reducing its environmental impact.

Community Waste Collection Initiatives


We firmly believe that creating a positive change starts at the grassroots level. Our community waste collection initiatives empower local communities to actively participate in waste management and recycling programs. Through education and collaboration, we aim to increase awareness of the importance of responsible waste disposal.

Environmental Awareness Campaigns


Plas-tech is dedicated to spreading awareness about the detrimental effects of plastic waste on our environment. Our environmental awareness campaigns strive to educate individuals, businesses, and communities about sustainable practices and the role they can play in curbing plastic pollution.
