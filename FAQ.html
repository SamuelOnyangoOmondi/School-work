Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We are fueling Africa, one home at a time.


1. What is Plas-tech?


Plas-tech is a pioneering start-up that focuses on converting plastic waste into clean cooking gas. Our innovative technology and sustainable practices allow us to tackle the pressing issue of plastic pollution while promoting eco-friendly energy solutions.

2. How does Plas-tech convert plastic waste to cooking gas?


Our conversion process utilizes advanced technology to transform plastic waste into clean cooking gas through a controlled and eco-friendly method. The process ensures efficient recycling while minimizing environmental impact.

3. What are the benefits of using Plas-tech's cooking gas?


Using Plas-tech's cooking gas not only reduces the reliance on traditional fossil fuels but also contributes to a cleaner environment by recycling plastic waste that would otherwise end up in landfills or oceans. By choosing our gas, you actively participate in sustainable energy practices and help combat plastic pollution.

4. Is Plas-tech's cooking gas safe to use?


Absolutely! Plas-tech adheres to rigorous safety standards and conducts regular quality checks to ensure the cooking gas we produce is safe for domestic and commercial use. Your safety is our priority.
