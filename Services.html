Welcome to our services page



At Plas-tech, we are dedicated to providing innovative and sustainable solutions to address the global challenge of plastic waste. Our range of services is designed to cater to various stakeholders and support our mission of creating a cleaner and greener future.




Our services


Plastic Waste Conversion Technology

Our flagship service revolves around cutting-edge technology that converts plastic waste into clean cooking gas. By utilizing advanced processes and state-of-the-art equipment, we transform plastic waste into a valuable resource, reducing its environmental impact and contributing to a circular economy.


Sustainable Waste Management Consulting

We offer expert consulting services to businesses, organizations, and government entities seeking to implement sustainable waste management practices. Our team of experts will assess your waste management processes, identify areas for improvement, and develop tailored strategies to minimize plastic waste generation.


Community Waste Collection Programs

Plas-tech actively engages with local communities to promote responsible waste disposal through our community waste collection programs. By fostering a culture of waste separation and recycling, we empower communities to play an active role in safeguarding the environment.


Green Energy Advocacy

As strong advocates for renewable energy, we promote the use of clean cooking gas derived from recycled plastic waste. Through our green energy advocacy, we raise awareness about the benefits of clean energy and its positive impact on the environment and society.


Educational Outreach

At Plas-tech, we believe that education is a vital component of driving sustainable change. Our educational outreach programs aim to raise awareness about plastic waste pollution and its consequences. We engage with schools, institutions, and community groups to impart knowledge about sustainable practices and inspire future environmental leaders.


Partnership Opportunities

We actively seek collaboration with organizations, businesses, and individuals who share our vision for a plastic-free future. Through strategic partnerships, we leverage collective expertise and resources to accelerate the adoption of sustainable solutions and create a more substantial impact.
